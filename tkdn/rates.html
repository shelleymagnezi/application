Rates

Check the rate calculator!


Our facility is available for rent for a one-time use or regularly on a recurring basis. Our fees are for the kitchen and storage. Kitchen rates range from $15 -$35 per hour depending on holiday/non-holiday peaks. Storage rates range from $10 - $75 depending on factors such as availability, kitchen usage, and standing reservations. Storage includes cooler and freezer shelves, dry storage, and equipment lockers. The rental costs include supplies for each client to clean the kitchen, equipment, and floors following their kitchen usage. Certified and licensed industrial commercial equipment deep cleaning service is performed in accordance with city and national codes and schedules.
